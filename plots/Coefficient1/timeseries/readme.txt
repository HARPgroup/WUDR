Plots rae generated from Time series Small Farmers script in Small_farmers_coeff folder.

Avg_ Coeff folder contains the plots for unreported withdrawals obtained by multiplying the average coefficent of census years to DEQ reported irrigtaion withdrawals from 2002-2017

Max_UnderTH: contains plots for all counties with census data and is obtained by assuming maximum Undet Th acerage from census years and applying deficit irrigation approch using PRISM dat a for all years from 2002-2017

Comparison time series are the plots for counties with irrigtaion withdrawals. The plots show time series using the rainfall deficit approach(max Undet Th) and the time series based on the annual values from Coefficients(avg)
